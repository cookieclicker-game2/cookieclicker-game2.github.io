About Cookie Clicker 2

Cookie Clicker 2 is the anticipated sequel to the original idle game. Your goal is simple: bake as many cookies as possible by clicking the giant cookie. Use your cookies to buy upgrades and buildings that bake cookies for you automatically.

The game introduces new features such as revamped graphics, more upgrades, and special golden cookies that grant temporary bonuses. It's the perfect game to play in the background while you work or study.

How to Play

  • Click: Tap the big cookie to bake cookies.
  • Upgrade: Buy cursors and grandmas to increase your CPS.
  • Unlock: Discover hidden achievements and rare upgrades.
  • Ascend: Reset your progress for heavenly chips and permanent boosts.
